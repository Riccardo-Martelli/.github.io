About me

Welcome to my website! I teach math, physics, and computer science to both high school and university students. My passion for science led me to pursue a degree in physics at the University of Milan, where I graduated with honors. Throughout the years, Iâ€™ve had the privilege of helping many students who struggled with scientific subjects, which inspired me to share my passion and support those in need. My teaching method is simple and straightforward: I avoid unnecessary, pompous explanations that often serve only to elevate the speaker (typically the professor) and instead focus on going straight to the point. I believe that providing a clear method is crucial for understanding the nature of a problem and approaching it in the most effective way.
Over the course of my career, I have worked with students with learning disabilities, such as DSA and ADHD, introducing precise methods and routines that make learning specific topics easier and help overcome any barriers they may face.
I graduated from an American high school and hold an IELTS C1 certification, so I can comfortably assist exchange students.

Why this website

One often has doubts about the expertise of the person he is referring to for help. Contrary to most other people, I've created this site to eliminate those doubts and answer some of the questions that students often ask me.

Method

My method reflects my personal inclination toward clarity and innovation. The best way to tackle a problem is to examine the theory, reformulate it in a simpler way, and then, by creating a functional method, solve the exercises easily and consolidate the acquired knowledge. To do so, I need all the necessary materials (notes, exercises, etc.) to understand the right course of action and achieve the desired result.
