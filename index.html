About me

Welcome to my website! I teach math, physics, and computer science to high school and university students. My passion for science lead me to study physics, so I enrolled at the University of Milan where I graduated with honors. Throughout the years I've helped many students that had struggled with scientific subjects so I decided to share some of my passion and help students in need. My method of teaching is quite simple I avoid pointless pompous explanations, that are exclusively to make the speaker (usually the professor) looks better, and go straight to the point. Giving a method is essential to understands the nature of the problem and to tackle it in the best way possible.
During my career I've helped people with learning disabilities such as DSA, ADHD, etc. a precise method and routine is introduced for these students so that learning specific topics become easier and every barrier, that might be present, is broken.
I've graduated from an american highschool and have a IELTS level of C1, hence I can easily help exchange students.
In this page you can find the courses I offer, a list of my publications and my contacts.

Why this website

One often has doubts about the expertise of the person he is referring to for help. Contrary to most other people, I've created this site to eliminate those doubts and answer some of the questions that students often ask me.

Method

My method reflects my personal inclination towards clarity and innovation. The best way to tackle a problem is to look at the theory, reformulate it in a new and simpler way, than, by creating a functional method, the exercises can be solved easily and the acquired knowledge is consolidated. To do so I need all the necessarily material (notes, exercises, etc.) to understand the right course of action in order to achieve the desired result.
