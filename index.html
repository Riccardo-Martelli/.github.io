Welcome to my website! I teach math, physics, and computer science to high school and university students. My passion for science lead me to study physics, so I enrolled at the University of Milan where I graduated with honors. Throughout the years I've helped many students that struggle with scientific subjects so I decided to share some of my passion and help students in need. My method of teaching is quite simple I avoid pointless pompous explanations, that usually are only to make the speaker (usually the professor) look better, and go straight to the point. Giving a method is essential to understands the nature of the problem and to tackle it in the best way possible.
During my career I've helped people with learning disabilities such as DSA, ADHD, etc. a precice method and routine is introduced for these students so that learning specific topics become easier and every barrier, that might be present, is broken.
